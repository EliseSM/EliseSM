About Me

At Capital One, I’ve worked across the full ML lifecycle — from designing data pipelines and anomaly detection models to deploying large-scale systems in production. Recently, I’ve been focused on cybersecurity applications of ML, including developing a RAG-based case summarization system for cyber analysts and building data drift detection models across 12+ critical threat datasets using Kubeflow and Databricks.

Previously, I designed time-series anomaly detection systems with Koopman operators, standardized large-scale cyber data pipelines, and developed low-latency APIs in Java and Python serving over 600,000 daily transactions in under 100ms.

I’m deeply interested in the intersection of machine learning, systems engineering, and cybersecurity, and I enjoy exploring how new technologies — from LLMs to distributed data platforms — can make enterprise systems more adaptive and transparent.
